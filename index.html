Victor Terhemba

Executive Director at Raising New Voices

Victor Terhemba is a civil society advocate. As a the programs officer at Raising New Voices Initiative he led the designs and implementations of several projects that promoted the inclusion and participation of young people in politics. He led the development and deployment of a civic-tech web-based platform that empowered citizens to hold public officials accountable. The platform was instrumental in the campaign for Electoral Reforms in Nigeria between 2021 and 2022. Terhemba is passionate about good governance in Nigeria and the development of young people. Victor Terhemba was the state publicity secretary (2016 – 2018) of KOWA party in Lagos state. He is also known as a political and public affairs commentor and has written several articles on political and social issues published in many Nigerian Newspapers and online news portals. He is also the producer and host of The Talk Shop – a TV talk show in Abuja. He is currently the Executive Director of Raising New Voices Initiative, a position he has hekd since 2022
